About Us

Hi everyone!

We just wanted to tell you all a little more about how we met. Chris and I attended UT Austin together, though for a few years we didn’t meet. In a school with 50,000 students, this may not shock you.

However, we happened to get the same scholarship given to two people in the university each year. We were invited to a few scholarship dinners, which is where Chris and I originally met. Eventually, we happened to also take a small seminar together, with only about 10 people in the class. After our seminar, I would always go to the gym. But one day when walking I heard a “Hey wait up!”, and there was Chris...from there the rest is history!

-Alexa

Austin

Austin is a young, vibrant city with an almost overwhelming amount of things to do. If you are looking for some outdoor fun, we recommend stand up paddleboarding (SUPing) or kayaking on Lady Bird Lake which splits the north and south parts of town.

Famous for being the "Live Music Capital of the World", you can always grab a drink and listen to music most nights of the week if you prefer the indoor scene. Listen to country at The White Horse . Relax to some jazz at The Elephant Room . Have an eclectic taste in music? Try out Sahara Lounge . Looking for more recommendations on things to eat and places to see? Just let me know and I'll point you in the right direction!

-Chris
