About Us
“Then love knew it was called love. And when I lifted my eyes to your name, suddenly your heart showed me my way” - Pablo Neruda

We attended UT Austin together, but didn't meet until we were half-way through college. In a school with 50,000 students, this may not shock you.

But in an act of serendipity, a scholarship given to two people in the university each year was awarded to both of us, and our friendship took root when we met at these scholarship-sponsored events. After attending a small seminar with only 10 students we grew even closer. Lunches became dinner dates, chit chat evolved into conversation and our friendship blossomed into a romantic relationship in January of 2012.

Since then we have been together for over six years, and are excited for you to celebrate the next chapter of our life with us!

Austin

Austin is a young, vibrant city with an almost overwhelming amount of things to do. If you are looking for some outdoor fun, we recommend stand up paddleboarding (SUPing) or kayaking on Lady Bird Lake which splits the north and south parts of town.

Famous for being the "Live Music Capital of the World", you can always grab a drink and listen to music most nights of the week if you prefer the indoor scene. Listen to country at The White Horse . Relax to some jazz at The Elephant Room . Have an eclectic taste in music? Try out Sahara Lounge . Looking for more recommendations on things to eat and places to see? Just let me know and we'll point you in the right direction!
